# Visa Requirements — Quick Reference (Demo Data)

> This is **synthetic demo content** for the Travel Concierge sample. It is not
> legal or immigration advice. Always confirm with official government sources.

## Schengen Area (Europe)

- Citizens of the US, Canada, UK, Australia, Japan and many others may enter the
  Schengen Area **visa-free for up to 90 days** within any 180-day period for
  tourism or business.
- A passport valid for **at least 3 months beyond** the planned departure date is
  required.
- From 2025, visa-exempt travellers must obtain an **ETIAS** travel authorization
  before arrival (a quick online form, valid for 3 years).

## United States

- Visa Waiver Program (VWP) travellers must hold an approved **ESTA** and a valid
  e-passport. Stays are limited to **90 days**.
- Travellers not eligible for the VWP generally need a **B-2 tourist visa**.

## Japan

- Many nationalities receive a **90-day visa exemption** on arrival for tourism.
- A **Visit Japan Web** registration speeds up immigration and customs.

## United Arab Emirates

- Many nationalities receive a **30- or 90-day visa on arrival**.
- Passport validity of **6 months** is required.

## General Tips

- Check passport validity rules early — some countries require 6 months.
- Keep proof of onward/return travel and accommodation.
- Some destinations require proof of sufficient funds or travel insurance.
